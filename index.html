high-resolution, high-sensitivity, high-accuracy, and high-throughput molecular measurements

Our State-of-the-art MS platforms support targeted and untargeted metabolomics, lipidomics, exposomics, and quantitative small-molecule measurement across complex biological and environmental samples.

Ion mobility MS

Bruker timsTOF HT

High-resolution LC-MS/MS with trapped ion mobility for deep molecular profiling and confident feature characterization.

High-throughput MS

SCIEX Echo ZenoTOF platform

High-efficiency mass spectrometry workflows for scalable molecular measurement across large sample sets.

High-resolution MS

Bruker impact II QTOF

Accurate-mass acquisition for untargeted metabolomics, unknown annotation, and method development.

Targeted quantitation

Thermo Quantis, Sciex QTRAP

Robust triple-quadrupole workflows for sensitive targeted measurement and validation of small molecules.
