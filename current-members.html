Amy Zheng

Amy joined the Huan Lab in May 2025 after earning her Ph.D. in Chemical Engineering from Vanderbilt University under Dr. Jamey Young. Her doctoral work applied metabolic flux analysis to microalgae to enhance biofuel production.

At the Huan Lab, she develops approaches to systematically quantify metabolic fluxes by integrating isotope tracing and untargeted metabolomics data.
